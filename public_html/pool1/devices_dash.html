NodaVare - ADS RFQ Response, Pool 1
Introduction

This web application is a working design and development prototype using datasets and APIs from openFDA. This prototype is being delivered by NodaVare Corporation's in response to the requirement in RFQ 4QTFHS150004, Agile Delivery Services (ADS I), Pool 1 for the U.S. General Services Administration.

Scenario - In this Pool 1 working prototype, we designed and built a web application to show the top devices with adverse reports since 2004. Further, you are able to see the top operators who were using the device at the time of the adverse event that may have caused the report. We understand from our user group that such graphs help view, understand and analyze the correlation between adverse reports on devices and its user and thus its use. We are hoping that in further sprints we can drill down further into what were the different effects of the adverse event on the operator, eg Hospitalization, Death etc.
